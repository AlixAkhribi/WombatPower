PROJECT WOMBAT

WHAT


Project Wombat retrieves real time data from the BLS (Bureau of Labor Statistics) and displays it back to the user in google maps with easy to read information. The way Project Wombat does this is by asking the user what industry they want to look up, which state and more specifically, which county. Once all these fields have been specified, our servers retrieve the data from the BLS and displays it on our Google Map API. To make the data even easier to read, we display it in more details on the right of the map.

WHO


Project Wombat is great for job seekers or current employees. Job Seekers can see which area around them has the most demand for their industry. One could even browse to see where demand is at an all-time high. Current employees can also benefit from Project Wombat by comparing the areas around them to the ones they are currently working in.

Businesses looking to strategize and expand, will benefit from Project Wombat. Finding where your business is growing is the key to success and seeing that data in real time diminishes chances of demand failure.
